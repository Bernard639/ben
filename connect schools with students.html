About SchoolConnect Rwanda

SchoolConnect Rwanda is a digital bridge designed to connect students, parents, and educational institutions across Rwanda. Our platform simplifies the school search and application process, making quality education more accessible through modern technology.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital hub for educational information in Rwanda, empowering every student to find their ideal academic path.

Our Mission

To streamline the enrollment process by providing transparent data and easy-to-use application tools, saving time for families and schools.

Why Choose SchoolConnect?

  • ✔️ Comprehensive Profiles: Detailed info on TVET, Secondary, and Primary schools.
  • ✔️ Online Applications: Submit your documents directly through our secure portal.
  • ✔️ Smart Search: Filter schools by name, category, or specialized courses.
  • ✔️ Verified Data: We work closely with institutions to ensure accurate information.
